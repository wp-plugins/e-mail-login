Log-in to your wordpress site using the e-mail address or classic username. Simply and efficient.

== Description ==

Tired of causing your members to remember their username when logging into your site? Now, give them a choice! With this plugin your members can log in into your website with their e-mail address OR with their username.

This simple and lightweight plugin will make the login process more efficient and easy. In many sites this function is integrated, but somehow Wordpress misses it, and now you can offer this possibility to your members!

Attention - After activating this plugin you can still log-in with your username!

= Translations included =

Italian -> Nome utente
French -> Identifiant

If you want your language included, please send the string to milesimarco [AT] outlook.com

== Installation ==
1. Upload our plug-in to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. All done! Now you can log-in with your e-mail address

== FAQ ==
1. Will i be able to log in with my username as normal?
Yes, you will. This plugin let you still log-in with your username. In fact, you will be asked to enter "Username or e-mail address".
